# 导师汇报视频脚本
> 格式：Part 1 镜头对着自己讲（约2-3分钟）→ Part 2 屏幕录制演示App（约3-4分钟）
> 语言：英文
> 最后更新：2026-04-11

---

## PART 1：镜头对着自己讲

### 【开场：问题是什么】

"This study is conducted in a rural primary school in China, with Grade 3 students aged 8-9. The class has 27 students, organized into approximately 13 pairs. Each pair shares one device — second-hand iPhones — so every student has access to the app simultaneously during class.

I am both the researcher and the English teacher of this class. This gives me direct access to observe, adjust, and reflect on every lesson.

The core problem is this: English is compulsory, but most students have almost zero exposure to English outside the classroom. As the teacher, I cannot provide individualized support for 27 students at the same time. And the students — they are afraid to speak."

---

### 【解决方案：不是"用了一个App"】

"My solution is not simply 'use an app.' It is a theoretically-grounded cooperative learning system, where two students share one device, take different roles — Student A and Student B — and must work together to complete each task.

The key word here is **must**. Not 'can cooperate' — **must cooperate**. Because I designed it so that neither student has enough information to finish alone."

---

### 【六个理论：不是贴标签，是设计约束】

"The app is built on six theoretical frameworks. But I want to be clear — I did not design the app first and then find theories to label it. The theories came first. Each theory answered a specific design question that I could not solve without it."

| Design Question | Theory |
|----------------|--------|
| Why must students cooperate? | Social Constructivism |
| How difficult should each task be? | SLA – Input Hypothesis (i+1) |
| Why must students talk to each other? | SLA – Interaction Hypothesis |
| Why must Student B speak English, not Chinese? | SLA – Output Hypothesis |
| Why split the task between A and B? | Cognitive Load Theory (CLT) |
| What happens when a student fails? | ZPD + Scaffolding |
| How do we keep students motivated? | Self-efficacy |

"Remove any one of these theories, and a specific part of the app's design collapses. This is what I mean by **blend** — not labeling, but structural dependency."

---

### 【一个决策，四个理论同时工作】

"Let me give you one concrete example. Why does Student A only see pictures, and Student B only see the English words?

This single design decision is constrained by four theories simultaneously:
- **Social Constructivism** — they must depend on each other
- **SLA Interaction Hypothesis** — the information gap creates real communicative need
- **SLA Output Hypothesis** — Student B must say the English word; saying it in Chinese won't work
- **CLT** — each student processes only half the information load

That is blend. One decision. Four theories. All necessary."

## PART 2：切换屏幕，打开App

### 【Step 1：进入App】
*操作：选学生姓名 → 进入 Listening → 站点1「听音接力」*

"Now let me show you how this works in practice. I'll walk you through one question in the Listening station."

---

### 【Step 2：A的屏幕】
*操作：点播放音频，指着图片选项*

"Student A sees an audio button and four pictures. No English words — only images.
This is CLT in action: we removed the text to reduce A's cognitive load.
A presses play, hears 'bear', and must choose the correct picture."

---

### 【Step 3：B的屏幕激活】
*操作：A选完，指B屏幕*

"The moment A answers, Student B's screen activates. B sees the picture A chose, and must select the Chinese meaning.

Notice: A could not do B's task — A was processing audio and images. B could not do A's task — B's screen was locked. This is Social Constructivism: the structure enforces cooperation."

---

### 【Step 4：答错，触发支架】
*操作：故意选错，展示四级支架*

"If the pair answers incorrectly, watch what happens.

Level 1: the app prompts them to discuss with each other first — this is ZPD, using the peer as a More Knowledgeable Other.

If they still fail, Level 2 provides a language hint. Level 3 gives more. Level 4 shows the complete answer.

This is Scaffolding: contingent, proportional, and temporary. The support fades as the student succeeds."

---

### 【Step 5：答对，Combo触发】
*操作：答对，看连击动画*

"When they answer correctly — especially consecutively — the app shows a combo animation and stars.

This is Bandura's Self-efficacy theory: mastery experiences, celebrated in the moment, build the student's belief that they **can** learn English."

---

### 【Step 6：下一题变难】
*操作：进入下一题*

"And the next question? The adaptive algorithm makes it slightly harder.

This is Krashen's i+1: always at the edge of what they can do — not too easy, not too hard. Personalized for this pair of students, in this moment."

---

### 【收尾】

"This is one question. Thirty seconds of interaction. Six theories, working at the same time — not as labels, but as the structural logic behind every element you just saw.

This is my answer to the question: how do I blend all selected theories to develop the app?"
